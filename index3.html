Personal Information

I’m a mature age student currently employed in the Education sector in IT. I have a rescue dog named Tom who is a Maltese Shih Tsu crossbreed. I live in Perth with my wife and two daughters. One of who starts her first year of University in 2020.

I’m also an Admin of a West Australian Gaming Community which was founded back in 2014. We have around 200 members and we regularly play together on both PlayStation and PC. Gaming is my main hobby, but I also enjoy watching Anime. My favorite game is Destiny, and my favorite Anime is One-Piece.

Interest in IT

My current interest in IT is Networking and Security as those facets are vital to my current employment. I also have a long running interest in the field of Gaming as that is something that’s always been a part of my life growing up.

It all started when my dad first brought home our very first computer. The Sinclair ZX Spectrum. I still remember loading my first game from a cassette tape using the command: Load “ “ That’s what started it all for me. I’ve been fascinated with IT since then.

If I had to attribute my spark to a person, it would have to be my dad. He was the one that introduced our very first computer into our home and he was the one that shared in my joy of gaming while growing up.
